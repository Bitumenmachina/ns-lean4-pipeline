## Data Files

### convergence/
- `convergence_summary.csv` ‚Äî Cross-resolution Œõ, K, œâ_max, T2/T1 at matched timesteps. Source: Phase 10b CSVs (`run_64.csv`, `run_128.csv`, `run_256.csv`).
- `convergence_table.md` ‚Äî Human-readable version of the same + V5 data.
- `convergence_overlay.png` ‚Äî Four-panel plot (Œõ, K, œâ_max, T2/T1 vs t).
- `convergence_assessment.md` ‚Äî Initial assessment (pre-M4, partially superseded by crease_invariants/).

### crease_invariants/
- `metrics.csv` ‚Äî M1‚ÄìM6 metric values for all 17 Phase 10b snapshots. Generated by `compute_metrics.py`.
- `product_robust.csv` ‚Äî Robust product statistics (sup, p99, p99_masked, top-100) for all snapshots. Generated 2026-04-12.
- `scaling_fit.csv` ‚Äî Power-law fits at gradient-peak voxel (Œ≤=0.86). Superseded by `scaling_fit_at_Pmax.csv` for the product-max analysis.
- `scaling_fit_at_Pmax.csv` ‚Äî Power-law fits at product-max voxel (Œ±‚âà0, Œ≤‚âà0, Œ≥=0.04). This is the primary scaling result.
- `m4_scaling.png` ‚Äî Log-log plot of Œõ, œâ, product vs N.
- `slice_maps.png` ‚Äî Six-panel diagnostic slice at N=256 t=20 z=œÄ.
- `paper_figure_invariant.png` ‚Äî Three-panel figure for paper ¬ß4.3 (Gaussian-smoothed, Gibbs-verified).
- `ring_check_slices.png`, `ring_check_profiles.png` ‚Äî Gibbs ringing verification across resolutions.
- `slices_N*_t*.npz` ‚Äî 2D slice data at z=œÄ for each snapshot (gitignored, regenerable).
- `compute_metrics.py` ‚Äî Script that regenerates all metric files from H5 snapshots.

### onset/
- `onset_table.csv` ‚Äî 13 V5 runs (Re=400‚Äì6400, N=64‚Äì256). Source: `reconnection_v5.db`.
- `re6400_extended.csv` ‚Äî 380-step timeseries for Re=6400 N=128 t=0‚Üí40. Source: `reconnection_v5.db` run_id=13.

### bridge/
